📚 Learn More About Cat Island

Cat Island in Shanghai is a place where stray and abandoned cats are cared for, fed, and loved by visitors. It has become a sanctuary for cats and a peaceful escape for humans to enjoy their company.
Visitors can play with cats, donate food, and support a non-profit organization!
